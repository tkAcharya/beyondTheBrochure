Hidden Truths
Scams, traps, local secrets
▼
Vending machine drinks are a tourist trap price-wise compared to konbini (convenience stores). Tipping is genuinely offensive — don't do it. The "all-you-can-eat" sushi places in tourist zones near Shibuya are low-quality frozen fish. The real sushi is at tiny counter spots in Shinjuku back alleys with no English menu.

Off-Radar Tips
Skip the clichés
▼
Skip Senso-ji on weekends. Instead, walk the Yanaka district on a Tuesday morning — it's old Tokyo that survived the war bombings, with actual locals. Shimokitazawa for vintage shopping and live indie music. And Koenji for the real Tokyo underground art scene.

Nearby Alternatives
If Tokyo feels like too much
▼
Kamakura is 1hr from Tokyo and far more zen — giant Buddha, forest trails, fewer tour groups. Hakone offers Mt Fuji views and onsen without the capsule hotel chaos. Both are day-trip or overnight worthy.
