Concept 1 — Neural Dark

Retains your brand colours

Keeps the existing rose/red accent (#f43f5e) and dark gradient base — feels like a premium upgrade, not a rebrand.

Verdict pinned at the top

The score chip (⚠️ 6.5/10) and blunt text is the first thing users see — reinforces the "no fluff" brand promise immediately.

Source tracker in the UI

Mini rows showing reddit, Lonely Planet, TripAdvisor scraped builds trust — users see exactly where the data came from.

2-column mini cards

Monsoon Risk + Budget side by side saves vertical space while keeping all 7 insight categories accessible without heavy scrolling.

Best for

Night-owl trip planners, digital nomads, Reddit-heavy users who already live in dark mode and want dense info fast.

Concept 2 — Clean Pro

References tab front-and-centre

Shows the full References view — your unique AI reliability scoring feature (the /rate-reference endpoint) gets much more visibility here.

All 10 sources as chips

The progress card shows all 10 scraped sources (Reddit → Atlas Obscura) as coloured chips — users instantly see the multi-source breadth that makes BtB trustworthy.

AI reliability scores displayed

Each reference card shows star rating + Gemini's reasoning + a score badge — turns the backend's /rate-reference into a visually compelling trust layer.

Light theme for focus

White cards on a light grey background keeps cognitive load low when reading detailed reliability reasoning — ideal for comparison scanning.

Best for

Mainstream users, casual travellers, people who prefer readability over aesthetics. Also strongest for product demos and screenshots.

Concept 3 — Bold Gradient

Destination-as-hero card

Bali's name is the focal point with a frosted glass card — makes the extension feel like a destination intelligence dashboard, not just a scraper tool.

Red Flags counter

A "7 Red Flags" stat right in the hero card is provocative and on-brand — it teases the hidden truths before users even scroll, driving engagement.

"Powered by Gemini" credibility

Showing "Gemini 2.0 Flash" and analysis time (3.2s) in the subtitle builds AI credibility — users know exactly what engine is giving them the truth.

Source reliability scores inline

Colour-coded rating pills (green/amber/red) on every source make the AI rating system scannable in seconds — no need to click into a separate tab.

Best for

Marketing screenshots, Product Hunt launches, social media demos — this is the concept that photographs best and creates the strongest first impression.
